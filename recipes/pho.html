Beef Pho

Description:

This beef pho features a comforting, richly-seasoned meat broth ladled over rice noodles and thinly sliced sirloin. Serve with hoisin, Sriracha, green onion, cilantro, bean sprouts, basil, and lime.

Recipe:

Begin by simmering beef bones, onion, ginger, and star anise in a pot of water for several hours to create a rich, fragrant broth. Strain, season with fish sauce and a touch of sugar, then ladle over fresh rice noodles, thinly sliced raw beef, and an assortment of herbs like Thai basil and cilantro. Top with bean sprouts, lime wedges, and sliced chili for that authentic, warming bowl of beef pho.

Ingredients:

  • Beef bones and tough beef cuts
  • Flat rice noodles
  • Aromatics (Ginger, onion (or shallot))
  • Spices (Star anise, cinammon/cassia sticks, clove)
  • Daikon
  • Salt, sugar, and fish sauce
  • Beef steak
