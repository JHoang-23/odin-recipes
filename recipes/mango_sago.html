Mango Sago

Description:

Mango Sago is a refreshing and satisfying summer dessert, with juicy chunks of mango and a mango/coconut milk tapioca pudding. The glug of heavenly condensed milk doesn't hurt either!

Recipe:

Boil sago pearls until translucent, then drain and rinse under cold water. Peel and dice ripe mango, blending some of it into a smooth puree. Stir the sago into a mixture of coconut milk, condensed milk, and the mango puree, then chill. Serve in bowls topped with fresh mango chunks and a drizzle of additional coconut milk.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup small-sized uncooked tapioca
  • 3 Ripe mangoes
  • 200ml unsweetened coconut milk
  • 60ml sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
